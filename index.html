Flights From Vancouver to Toronto

What’s the Pattern in These Flights?

Are you a post-secondary student, with home in Vancouver, but school in Toronto? Have you ever wondered how the prices fluctuate over the year? We have done the research for you, for the year of 2026!

Use Arrow Key or Scroll Down!

Why does it matter?

Domestic flights have stayed unaffordable even after COVID-19.

According to a CBC article published in July 2025, air travel industry in Canada is still recovering from financial losses during COVID-19 pandemic. When faced with growing demand despite inflation and increased costs of operations, the flights have become increasingly expensive in 2025. We imagined that this would pose stress on students who have to regularly take domestic flights.

These visualizations would encourage informed choices.

The goal of this visualization is to help frequent travellers to Toronto make more strategic choices when planning their trips.

Where are we pulling the data from?

We have built the dataset by ourselves!

We did not have a dataset sourced online as we were not able to find any dataset that lists daily flight prices.The data have been manually collected from Google Flights and SkyScanner search platforms.
